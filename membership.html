Membership

Becoming a member of the P(CC)2 is an excellent way to make connections with others who share a common interest in cybersecurity and quickly grow your computing knowledge through constant collaboration and practical application of skills learned in and out of the classroom. If you're not sure then come spend some time with us on Discord and find out for yourself.

Steps To Apply

  1. Contact our treasurer via email or on Discord at Redx#7194 about providing a minimum $25 donation to the club.
    • Donations and club funds go toward events and projects that make membership valuable. All fundraising (including membership donations) is managed by the ASPCC on behalf of the club.
  2. Complete the Membership Application
  3. Watch for your membership packet in your email within 48 hours!
